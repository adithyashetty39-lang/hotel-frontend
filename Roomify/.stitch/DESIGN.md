# Design System: Roomify Hotel Management Dashboard

## Aesthetics & Vibe
- **Vibe:** Clean, modern, organic, and data-driven. Very premium and spacious.
- **Reference:** "Grow Smart. Trade Fresh." UI (fresh, natural aesthetic adapted for a professional SaaS dashboard).

## Color Palette
- **Primary:** Vibrant 'Mint/Leaf' Green (`#10B981` or `#2ecc71`). Used for primary CTAs, active status, and positive indicators.
- **Secondary:** Earthy/Warm accents (soft terracotta or warm wood) for high-end feel and premium room types.
- **Surface & Backgrounds:** Clean off-white (`#F9FAFB`) for main app background to ensure high contrast, with crisp white (`#FFFFFF`) for elevated cards and modals.
- **Alerts:** Cool blues for data/revenue, warm oranges for occupied/warnings, soft red for maintenance/errors.

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (or Outfit, Plus Jakarta Sans). Modern, geometric Sans-Serif.
- **Data Font:** Highly legible tabular numbers for financial data, booking dates, and KPIs.

## Shapes & Geometry
- **Corners:** Soft, friendly rounded corners (12px - 16px radius) across all cards, inputs, and buttons.
- **Elevation:** Soft, diffuse drop shadows rather than harsh dark shadows.

## Web Layout Guidelines
- Widescreen desktop dashboard orientation (Sidebar + Main Content area).
- **Global Navigation (Sidebar):** Left-aligned, persistent. Contains Dashboard, Front Desk, Bookings, Room Directory, Guests, Finance.
- **Top Bar:** Global Search input, Notification Bell, User Role/Profile Badge.

## Component Specifics (The Dashboard)
- **Metrics Row:** Modern stat cards featuring sparkline charts for Occupancy Rate, Arrivals, and Revenue.
- **Room Status Widget:** A rich visual grid of "Room Cards" featuring an image of the room, current status (Green/Orange/Red dot). Hover states and energetic micro-interactions.
